Where does our milk come from?

Mapping flows from milk processing plants to five retail sites in the Hanover, NH and West Lebanon, NH. When read against the backdrop of a crisis-prone dairy industry, the relatively regional nature of milk processing (not withstanding specialty milks like organic or lactose-free) reveals the limitations of the "buy local" framework as a way to build just food systems.
